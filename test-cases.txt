Test Suite: Hotel Booking UI Verification
App URL: https://automationintesting.online/

TC_01: Verify successful room booking with valid data
Pre-conditions: User is on the homepage.
Steps:
1. Navigate to the "Book this room" section.
2. Select specific dates on the calendar (e.g., drag and drop 2 nights).
3. Fill in "First Name" with "Dmytro".
4. Fill in "Last Name" with "Skaliush".
5. Fill in "Email" with "dmytro@test.com".
6. Fill in "Phone" with "12345678901".
7. Click the "Book" button.
Expected Result:
- A success modal/message appears: "Booking Successful!".
- The booked dates are no longer selectable on the calendar.

TC_02: Verify booking fails with invalid/missing data
Pre-conditions: User is on the homepage.
Steps:
1. Navigate to the "Book this room" section.
2. Select dates on the calendar.
3. Leave "First Name", "Last Name", "Email", and "Phone" fields EMPTY.
4. Click the "Book" button.
Expected Result:
- Error messages are displayed under the respective fields (e.g., "Firstname must not be blank").
- Booking is not processed.

TC_03: Verify unavailability of already booked dates
Pre-conditions: A booking already exists for dates X to Y.
Steps:
1. Refresh the page to update the calendar view.
2. Observe the dates X to Y on the calendar.
3. Attempt to select dates X to Y.
Expected Result:
- The dates are visually distinct (e.g., red or grayed out).
- User cannot select these dates for a new booking.
